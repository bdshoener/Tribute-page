Satoru Iwata

The man who redefined Nintendo

Satoru Iwata was a Japanese video game programmer and businessman who served as the fourth president and chief executive officer of Nintendo.

A timeline of Iwata's life:

  • 1959 - Born in Sapporo, Japan
  • 1983 - Began working for HAL Laboratories
  • 1992 - Promoted to president of HAL
  • 1999 - Rewrote code for Pokemon Gold and Silver, allowing both Johto and Kanto to be played
  • 2000 - Hired by Nintendo as head of the Corporate Planning Division at NCL
  • 2002 - Promoted to CEO of Nintendo
  • 2013 - Promoted to CEO of Nintendo of America
  • 2015 - Death at age 55

"On my business card, I am a corporate president. In my mind, I am a game developer. But in my heart, I am a gamer."

Satoru Iwata
